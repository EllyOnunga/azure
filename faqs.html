General info

Azure Bay is located on Mombasa Road, Nairobi, right next to the Capital Center. Our waterfront-inspired setting offers a serene escape from the city's hustle, perfect for a relaxing dining experience.

We are currently open from Monday to Saturday, 08:00 AM to 05:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays to allow our team to rest and prepare for another week of serving the finest seafood in Nairobi.

Yes, Azure Bay is fully wheelchair accessible. We have ramps at the entrance and spacious seating arrangements to ensure all our guests enjoy a comfortable and inclusive dining experience.

Booking Policy

You can easily make a reservation through our website's booking page. Alternatively, you can call us directly or send a message via WhatsApp using the button on our site.

Currently, there is no cancellation fee. However, we kindly ask that you notify us at least 2 hours in advance if your plans change, so we can accommodate other guests waiting for a table.

Absolutely! We love hosting celebrations. For private events, corporate functions, or large group bookings (10+ people), please contact us via our contact form or phone to discuss custom menu options and space availability.

For standard dining, we don't have a minimum spend. However, for exclusive use of our private dining areas or for large corporate events, a minimum spend requirement may apply depending on the date and time. Please reach out to our events team for a detailed quote.

Menu details

Yes, we take food allergies very seriously. Please inform your server about any allergies before ordering. While we handle all ingredients with care, please note that our kitchen processes shellfish, nuts, and gluten.

We pride ourselves on serving only the freshest catch. Most of our seafood is sourced daily from local sustainable fisheries at the coast and brought to Nairobi using chilled transport to maintain peak quality.

Yes, all our seafood is Halal-compliant. We use high-quality, Halal-certified ingredients in our kitchen preparation to ensure our menu is accessible to everyone in our diverse community.

Delivery support

We offer delivery to most parts of Nairobi, including South B, South C, Upper Hill, CBD, and Syokimau. Delivery fees vary based on distance from our location on Mombasa Road.

Our average delivery time is 30 to 60 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions. We prioritize quality of service to ensure your food arrives at the perfect temperature.

We strive for punctuality, but if your order is delayed beyond the estimated time, please call us at +254 102 880 577. Our team will immediately track your delivery and provide you with a live update.
